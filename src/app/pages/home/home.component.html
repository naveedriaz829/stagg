His Royal Highness Crown Prince Mohammed Bin Salman announced the Sustainable Tourism Global Center (STGC) in 2021. The center is the world’s first multi-country, multi-stakeholder global coalition that will lead, accelerate and track Travel & Tourism industry’s journey to a sustainable future.

Why now?

A global climate
and biodiversity crisis

Climate change is a global crisis, described as "a code red for humanity" by the UN Secretary General. No place or community is immune, with disadvantaged communities, including Travel & Tourism destinations, being particularly vulnerable.

Rising temperatures drive environmental degradation, natural disasters, and food and water insecurity. Sea levels are rising, coral reefs are dying, and biodiversity is declining. Despite global commitments, progress is too slow.
