Our Mandate

Lead, accelerate, and track the travel and tourism industry's transition to sustainable practices, while driving action to protect nature and support communities

Our Vision

To lead the tourism sector in becoming a force for sustainable development and effective in reducing environmental impact, while remaining profitable and sustainable as an industry

Our Mission

Deliver applied & impact driven research, stakeholder centric services and end-to-end solutions, to enable the industry’s stakeholders to accelerate their transition and achieve the global and the industry sustainability goals

Leading for thePositive Impact

The STGC is uniting the travel sector to protect nature and support communities. Our mission is to be the leading global center for sustainable Travel & Tourism, offering impact-driven research, stakeholder services, and solutions.

We bring together public and private sector leaders—countries, businesses, NGOs, and academia— to provide tools for micro, small, and medium businesses, travelers, and destinations to drive lasting change.

To drive sustainable tourism, STGC is launching a Global Research Center for Climate, Nature & Communities. The Center will hire top researchers and collaborate with leading universities and organizations to provide actionable insights, tools, and solutions.
